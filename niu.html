A Love Letter for Myself
by: Cedric Niu

Dear my beloved Self,

Happy Valentines Day my beloved self! Thank you for being the person you truly are. I could fairly say that being the best version of oneself is being true to yourself and you have remained that way. You have brought countless joyuous memories to my life and I am so grateful to you for that. i would be nothing without you. Thank you so much for everything. Always remember that continue to stay to who you are. Happy Valentines Day!!!

Sincerely Yours,

Cedric Niu,
